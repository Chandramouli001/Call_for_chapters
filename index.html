About the Book

Tiny Machine Learning (TinyML) enables machine learning models to run on ultra-low power embedded devices and IoT systems. This edited book presents the latest research developments, architectures, and applications of TinyML technologies across various domains including healthcare, smart cities, agriculture, and industrial IoT.

The book aims to bring together researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to share innovative ideas and real-world implementations in TinyML powered IoT systems.

Key Topics

  • Introduction to TinyML
  • Edge Intelligence for IoT Devices
  • TinyML for Smart Healthcare
  • TinyML in Wearables
  • Industrial IoT Applications
  • Smart Agriculture Systems
  • Neural Networks for Ultra Low Power Devices
  • Model Compression & Optimization
  • Energy Efficient TinyML
  • Security and Privacy in TinyML
